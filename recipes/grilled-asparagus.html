Grilled Asparagus Recipe

Description

Searching for the best summer side dish that comes together in minutes? Look no further than this easy grilled asparagus thatâ€™s tossed in olive oil, seasoned with salt and pepper, and grilled until perfectly tender.

Ingredients

  • 1lb fresh asparagus
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  2. Step 2

    Lightly coat the asparagus spears with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Step 3

    Cook asparagus on the preheated grill, turning often until lightly charred and fork-tender about 3 to 5 minutes.
